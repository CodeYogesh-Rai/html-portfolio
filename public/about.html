About Me

Hello! I’m Yogesh Rai, an aspiring electrical engineer with a strong foundation in power distribution, auto electrical systems, and electrical infrastructure maintenance. Currently, I am completing my Diploma in Electrical Engineering under CTEVT, with plans to further my education through a bachelor’s degree. I have gained hands-on experience in electrical system operation and maintenance through a 6-month internship at Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA), Kuleshwor, where I worked alongside professionals to troubleshoot, optimize, and enhance power distribution networks. My expertise extends to fault diagnosis, grid inspections, and system efficiency improvements, making me well-equipped to contribute to Nepal’s growing energy sector. Beyond electrical engineering, I have specialized training in auto electric systems, certified by SIPRADI, Kathmandu Metropolitan, which has broadened my technical skill set in vehicle electrical diagnostics and maintenance. I am passionate about innovation, problem-solving, and technological advancements in the electrical field. Whether it’s optimizing power distribution systems, diagnosing faults, or improving efficiency, I strive to make a meaningful impact through my work.
